Valerie Mulcahy

Valerie Mulcahy is an Irish footballer. As a Ladies' Gaelic footballer, she played for Cork, winning ten All-Ireland titles and nine Ladies' National Football League titles between 2005 and 2015. She was also an All Star on six occasions. As a women's association football player, she represented Ireland at the 2003 Summer Universiade and played for Cork City in the Women's National League.
